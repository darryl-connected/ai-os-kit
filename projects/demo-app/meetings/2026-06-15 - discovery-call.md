## Meeting Purpose

Discovery call with internal stakeholders on the proposed Habit Tracker mobile app. Establish scope, success metrics, and initial user personas.

## Key Takeaways

- **Target user:** working professionals (25-40) who want a low-friction way to build daily habits. Existing solutions feel like "spreadsheets with streaks."
- **Success metrics:** D30 retention > 25%, daily active users / monthly active users (DAU/MAU) > 20%, app store rating > 4.5.
- **Initial scope:** habit creation, daily check-in, streak visualization, weekly summary. No social features in v1.
- **Out of scope:** integrations (Apple Health, Google Fit), premium tier, web version.

## Topics

### Target users

- Primary: working professionals 25-40, urban, time-poor, value efficiency
- Secondary: parents who want to model habits for kids
- Excluded for v1: teens, hardcore self-trackers (Quantified Self segment)

### Success metrics

- **Retention:** D30 > 25% (industry average is ~15% for utility apps)
- **Engagement:** DAU/MAU > 20% (sticky app, not just installed)
- **Satisfaction:** App Store rating > 4.5, NPS > 40

### Initial scope (v1)

- Habit creation (name, frequency, time-of-day)
- Daily check-in (one-tap confirm)
- Streak visualization (current + best)
- Weekly summary (notification Sunday 8pm)

### Out of scope (deferred)

- Integrations (Apple Health, Google Fit)
- Social features (friends, accountability partners)
- Premium tier / monetization
- Web version
- Advanced analytics (correlation, habit stacking)
